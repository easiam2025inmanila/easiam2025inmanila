What is EASIAM2025?

EASIAM2025 is the 18th East Asia SIAM Section Conference that will take place at De La Salle University in Manila, Philippines from June 30-July 3, 2025. This conference brings together researchers specializing in industrial and applied mathematics, focusing on science, engineering, and technology. The conference encompasses all facets of applied mathematics as it pertains to science, industry, engineering, and technology in East and Southeast Asia.
